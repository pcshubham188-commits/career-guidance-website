What is a Stock Trader?

A stock trader buys and sells shares for short-term or long-term profit. It requires market analysis and risk control.

Eligibility & Skills

  • No formal degree needed but commerce/finance background helps
  • Skills: Technical analysis, chart reading, discipline, patience

How to Start

  1. Open a Demat + Trading account (Zerodha, Upstox)
  2. Learn from Zerodha Varsity, YouTube, or paid courses
  3. Practice with virtual trading apps
  4. Start small and grow gradually

Certifications

  • NISM Series VIII (Equity Derivatives)
  • NISM Series VA (Mutual Funds)
