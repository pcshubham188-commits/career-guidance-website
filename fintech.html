What is Fintech?

Fintech = Finance + Technology. This industry builds digital platforms for investing, payments, insurance, loans, and more.

Popular Fintech Startups in India

  • Zerodha (Stock trading)
  • Groww, Paytm Money (Investments)
  • CRED, PhonePe, BharatPe (Payments & Credit)

Skills Required

  • Coding (HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Python)
  • Understanding of banking & finance
  • UI/UX design + product thinking

How to Start?

  1. Learn fintech basics from platforms like Coursera, edX
  2. Join a startup or intern in a fintech company
  3. Build your own product (like an app or tool)
  4. Apply for Startup India or incubators
