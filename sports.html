⚽ Sports Career - Roadmap

Overview

Sports is not only a passion but also a full-time career for many. With proper training and exposure, one can build a career as an athlete or in allied fields.

Career Options

  • Professional Athlete (Cricket, Football, Athletics, etc.)
  • Coach / Fitness Trainer
  • Sports Physiotherapist
  • Referee / Umpire / Official
  • Sports Analyst or Journalist

How to Start

  • Join a sports academy or coaching center
  • Participate in district, state, and national-level tournaments
  • Apply for trials (AIFF, BCCI, state-level federations)

Top Institutions in India

  • Netaji Subhas National Institute of Sports (NSNIS), Patiala
  • SAI (Sports Authority of India) Training Centers
  • LNIPE, Gwalior (Physical Education University)

Average Earnings

Entry-level earnings start from ₹10,000/month. Star players, league athletes, or national team members earn in lakhs/month + sponsorships.

Tips

  • Start training young (age 10–14 is ideal)
  • Focus on physical fitness, discipline, and consistency
  • Build a strong portfolio and participate in maximum events
