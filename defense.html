What are Defense Services?

The Indian Armed Forces include the Army, Navy, and Air Force. A career in defense offers pride, discipline, respect, and job security.

Entry Routes (After 12th & Graduation)

  • NDA (National Defence Academy): After 12th, for Army, Navy & Air Force (Only male candidates, female entry under process)
  • CDS (Combined Defence Services): After graduation, for officer-level posts in Army, Navy, and Air Force
  • AFCAT: Air Force Common Admission Test (Flying, Technical, Ground Duty)
  • TES/UES/SSC-Tech: For Engineering students directly into technical branches
  • INET: Indian Navy Entrance Test
