⚖ Civil Services - Career Roadmap

What are Civil Services?

Top government jobs like IAS, IPS, and IFS that shape administration and policy-making at all levels of government.

Main Exams

  • UPSC CSE (Union Public Service Commission) – For IAS, IPS, IFS, IRS
  • State PSC Exams – Like MPSC, BPSC, MPPSC, KPSC for state-level services

Eligibility

  • Any graduate (any stream)
  • Age limit: 21–32 years (general), relaxation for OBC/SC/ST

Exam Pattern (UPSC)

  1. Prelims – MCQs (GS + CSAT)
  2. Mains – 9 written papers (Essay, GS, Optional)
  3. Interview – Personality Test

Top Coaching / Resources

  • UPSC Official Website
  • NCERT books, Laxmikanth (Polity), Spectrum (History)
  • Coaching: Vision IAS, Drishti, Insights IAS

Perks & Salary

Starting: ₹56,100/month + perks (vehicle, house, staff, DA). High power, respect, and career growth.
