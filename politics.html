Overview

A career in politics allows individuals to contribute to society, influence decision-making, and serve the nation or local communities.

How to Start a Career in Politics

  • Join student unions (e.g., NSUI, ABVP) during college
  • Participate in local governance, community service, and NGOs
  • Work for or with an elected leader or political party
  • Stand in local elections (Municipality, Panchayat, ZP)
